PATH AND CURVES (ADVANCED)
==================================================

This is advanced - for cutting/plotting operations

CURVE CONCEPTS:
• Node - point on a curve
• Segment - line between nodes
• SubPath - connected segments
• Curve - collection of subpaths

Created red curved line

SHAPE COMBINING (Boolean):
• Weld - combines shapes into one
• Trim - cuts one shape from another
• Intersect - keeps only overlap
• Combine - merges with hole

CONVERSIONS:
• ConvertToCurves - makes editable
• ConvertToBitmap - rasterizes
• ConvertOutlineToObject

USED FOR:
• Cutting machines (vinyl, laser)
• CNC routing
• Advanced shape editing
